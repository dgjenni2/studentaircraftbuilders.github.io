November 8th, 2013

Skeleton Assembly and Attempt to Attach Skin

Builders: Andreas Brunner, Jeff Schroeder, Medhi Saeed
Foreman: Andy Myrna

During this session, the ribs were match drilled and the skeleton was assembled as shown below in Figure 1.

The L-angles which attach to the spar and the end ribs ended up not fitting within the end ribs due to the curvature of the rib. To remedy this, one of the corners was rounded. Figures 2 and 3 illustrate this. This is a common occurrence for fitting L-angles into ribs.

The next step was to cleco the skin to the skeleton (Figure 4) and match drill the holes to the spar and ribs. However, due to the holes not aligning as shown in Figure 5 and a lack of time, we were unable to drill any holes.
