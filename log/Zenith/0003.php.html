December 4th, 2013

Attaching Skin and Match Drilling

Builders: Andreas Brunner, Jessie Lauzon, Kevin Kang
Foreman: James Toothaker and Andy Myrna

This was a last ditch effort to finish the horizontal stabilizer before the winter break. We were finally able to get the spar center line and skin holes to line up. The holes were drilled to size #30 as shown in Figure 1. Due to rushing, we neglected to make sure the ribs were aligned as well. These were badly off center (Figure 2) and a replacement order for the two right ribs and nose ribs were made.
