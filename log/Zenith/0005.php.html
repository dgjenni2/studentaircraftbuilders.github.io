February 21st, 2014

Match Drilling New Ribs

Builders: Jessie Lauzon, Michael Shemanske
Foreman: Andy Myrna

When picking up the rest of the airframe kit, we also received the replacement ribs for the horizontal stabilizer. The first step was to match drill the 75T1-3 ribs to the spars as seen in Figure 1.

The same thing had to be done for the nose ribs. They are shown attached to the front spar in Figure 2.

After this, we were able to get the skin back on using ratchet straps (Figure 3) and, with multiple attempts, were able to get all of the ribs' centerlines to line up with the holes in the skin. We were then able to match drill the new ribs to the skin.
